Working process
Get in touch
[STEP-1]
Consultation & Discovery
Every project begins with an in-depth conversation to understand the client’s needs, lifestyle, and vision. This step defines the goals, budget, and desired atmosphere of the space.
[STEP-2]
Development & Coordination
Once the design is approved, detailed plans and specifications are created. Coordination with suppliers, contractors, and artisans ensures every detail is executed with precision.
[STEP-3]
Concept & Design
Moodboards, sketches, and 3D models are developed to visualize the project. Materials, colors, and layouts are explored to craft a unique and functional concept.

Delivery & Styling
The final stage brings the project to life, with each detail carefully placed to create a harmonious and functional interior.

FAQ
Get in touch
What types of projects do you work on?
I design both residential and commercial spaces, from apartments and houses to offices and retail stores. Each project is customized to reflect the client’s needs and lifestyle.
How does the design process work?
It begins with a consultation to understand your vision, followed by concept development, design planning, and coordination until the final installation and styling.
Do you work with specific budgets?
Yes, I adapt each project to the client’s budget, carefully selecting materials, furniture, and solutions that balance aesthetics, functionality, and cost-effectiveness.
Can you work remotely or only in Toulouse?
While I am based in Toulouse, I also offer remote design services and can collaborate on projects across France and internationally.
How long does a typical project take?
The timeline depends on the scope. Small projects may take a few weeks, while larger renovations can span several months, including planning and execution.
Do you also help with furniture and material selection?
Yes, I assist with sourcing and selecting furniture, finishes, and materials to ensure everything aligns with the overall concept and style of your space.
